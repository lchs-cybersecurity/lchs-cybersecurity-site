Veritas

About

Veritas is an enterprise-grade phishing detection software system. Through a universal whitelist/blacklist, refined email/website safety rating algorithms, and a simple but powerful end-user interface, Veritas makes phishing attempts against your organization easy to identify and streamlines the process of responding to them.

Plans & Pricing

Enterprise Edition
  • Access to site-checking and email-checking algorithms, administration interface, phishing email report system, organization-level blacklist/whitelist
  • $0.25 per member, per month

Individual Edition
  • Access to basic site-checking
  • Available free of charge
