What do we do?

We're a group of students who work in coordination with the District Tech Department to keep LCUSD's students and staff members safe from threats to our IT security. In an age where public and private institutions are increasingly becoming the victims of targeted cyber attacks, it's up to us to stay ahead of the curve and keep LCUSD secure.

Projects
phishy

phishy is a series of mockup phishing emails sent out in order to assess how the staff of LCUSD would respond to real-world phishing attacks. Information gathered from staff responses allows the Tech Department to more effectively allocate certain technologies where they need to as well as personalize staff training on an individual basis.

Veritas Enterprise Edition

Veritas Enterprise Edition is a browser extension designed to help to protect organizations from email and website phishing attempts. It does so by augmenting the Gmail interface by adding accessible controls for reporting phishing and verifying incoming emails with a customizable blacklist and whitelist, as well as an organization-wide blacklist/whitelist. Veritas currently supports the Gmail web client.
